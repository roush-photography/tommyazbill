About Tommy Azbill / Photographer

_________________________________________________________________________

Something magical happened to me one day many years ago.
I took a picture of my kids with a used 35mm film camera that I
bought from a friend. When I saw the photographs I had taken and looked
through the prints I was hooked instantly. I very quickly found out
that this new-found interest in photography was more than just a hobby,
it had grown quickly into a passion. Since then I have taken thousands of photos.
It is still a passion today. I have travelled to many places and have
seen many things taking pictures all along the way.

There is no other way to explain it other than to say very simply, "I LOVE PHOTOGRAPHY".

If you need photographs, please let me share my passion with you and your family.
